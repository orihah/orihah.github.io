Our dog, Sparky, likes to eat whatever the kids are snacking on. Is it OK for the dog to eat chocholate?
Chocolate is toxic to dogs. Please do not feed your dog chocolate. Try playing a game with your children – when you feed them people treats, the can feed Sparky dog treats.
