On this page you will find the videos for each boss and link fight descritions used to create our raid strategies. Watching these videos and going over the fight descriptions will help prepare you for each fight in the current raid.

Champions of the Light

For this fight on all 3 difficulties our team currently uses a ZERG strategy as noted in the fight description. This means DPS will ignore adds other than to interrupt Angelic Renewal. Healers may also need to save cooldowns for the end of the fight.


Wowhead's Fight Description

Grong

The main difference on mythic here is that 2 adds spawn and each drops on orb on death. We currently have a single DPS handling the orbs, to do this we must ensure that the second add dies around the same time the first orb is taken. We use the orbs primarly during the bosses Tank mechanic to prevent raid damage.


Wowhead's Fight Description

Jadefire Masters

The main element to remember here is that all but 2 people, 1 healer and 1 tank, will be pulled into the sky to complete Multi-sided Strike. During this each player will be placed in the center of a circle and 5 enemies will appear around the circle in sequence, kicking through the character after a short time. The player must be facing the monk when it kicks or take high damage. The color order of the monks is the same each time, Blue Green Red Green Blue. We will also assign orbs to specific players for the guantlet phase, if you are assigned a orb famaliarize yourself with its placement.


Wowhead's Fight Description

Opulence

The main difficulty to this fight is that all dps must learn both hallways, as they will be swapped back and forth throughout the fight. To make each room easier orbs and lasers should be positioned around the outside of the room. Learning the duration and speed of Flames of Punishment will also make survivabilty much easier. Any deaths during the hallway phase of the fight is almost always a cause for a wipe. DPS must be close to even for each hallway, as each side must reach its gem room within a similar timeframe to allow healers to deal with Theif's Bane.


Wowhead's Fight Description

Conclave of the Chosen

The only significant change to this fight on mythic is that a curse is placed on random characters throughout the fight. The curse Bwonsamdi's Wrath prevents the cursed player from receiving any healing and when it is removed it moves to a new character. By the end of the fight there will be 5-6 of these debuffs cycling throughout the raid. As the only classes that can remove curses are Druids, Shamans and Mages, we recommend that ALL players of these classes download Decursive this addon will assist in removing the curse. Only removing when the player is low health or during Paku's Wrath.
