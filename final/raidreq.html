Raid Requirements

Here you will find a list of addons required for raid

  • Exorsus

    This addon will allow the raid to receive specialized notes and visuals to assist in fight strategies.

  • WeakAuras 2

    This addon allows you to create specialized alerts and timers for encounters or your class. It is required so that you can import raid required alerts from the group.

  • Decursive

    This addon is only required for healers and Druids/Shamans/Mages for removing curses during the Conclave fight.

Raid Recommended Addons

Here you will find a list of addons recommended for raid

  • Deadly Boss Mods

    This addon has preset alerts for boss and dungeon mechanics. It is highly recommended, and if continued mistakes are made by a player they maybe required to download.

  • BigWigs Bossmods

    May be used as an alternative to the above.

  • Healbot

    This addon will assist healers in raid by allowing single clicks to heal raid members rather than the use of a key bind.

  • Vuhdo

    Similar to above.
